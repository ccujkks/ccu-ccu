OUR TECHNOLOGY

Engineering The Future Of Carbon Management

Our modular Carbon Capture & Utilization platform integrates industrial carbon capture, purification, compression and mineralization into one seamless ecosystem.

Point Source Capture

Industrial exhaust gases are captured directly from cement manufacturing plants before carbon dioxide reaches the atmosphere.

Gas Purification

Advanced filtration removes dust, moisture and impurities, producing high-purity CO₂.

Compression

Purified carbon dioxide is compressed for safe storage and efficient transportation.

Mineralization

CO₂ reacts with calcium compounds to permanently lock carbon inside concrete.

PROCESS

How Carbon Becomes Concrete

01

Industrial CO₂ Collection

CO₂-rich exhaust gases are collected from cement plants before release into the atmosphere.

02

Cleaning & Purification

Dust, nitrogen, moisture and unwanted gases are removed to produce high-purity carbon dioxide.

03

Compression

The purified gas is compressed to industrial pressure, making storage and utilization more efficient.

04

Concrete Production

Compressed CO₂ reacts with cement compounds, forming calcium carbonate inside fresh concrete.

05

Permanent Carbon Storage

The captured carbon becomes part of the concrete itself, creating stronger, carbon-negative materials.
